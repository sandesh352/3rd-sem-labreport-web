About BCA Course

Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) is a four-year, 8-semester undergraduate program of Tribhuvan University (TU) under the faculties of Humanities and Social Science. From the academic year, 2074/2075 TU launched this program. Firstly, this BCA program was launched only in six government colleges so-called constitute the campus of Tribhuvan University with an allocation of 35 seats for each campus.

Objectives of BCA Course

  • To provide practical-based knowledge related to computer applications.
  • To explore skills and techniques of students in software industries with problem-solving and managing skills.
  • To provide the base to students for their further studies after graduation.

Admission and Eligibility Criteria

Admission Criteria :

  • Students should collect and submit the admission form to their interested college . Generally, the admission form opens every year in the month of Bhadra/Ashoj.
  • No need to fill the form from different colleges, one form is sufficient to be eligible to attend the entrance exam for BCA. More than one form can be invalid.

Eligibility Criteria :

  1. Minimum D grade in each subject of grade 11 and 12 with a CGPA 1.8 or more
  2. Minimum score of second division marks in 10+2, PCL, or equivalent in any discipline.
  3. Students who have passed grade 11 and are waiting for grade 12 results can also apply. However, they have to submit all the required documents at the time of admission.

Scope and job prospects

BCA graduate students have great scope and job opportunities in the context of Nepal. As already mentioned in the context of Nepal, BCA graduated students can have a high chance of exploring themselves all over the world with their skills and technical and problem-solving skills and also have a chance to get hired from different highly reputed companies.

  1. Web Designer
  2. Computer and Information System Manager
  3. Web Developer
